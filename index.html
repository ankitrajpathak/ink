Help & Documentation - INK
What is INK?
INK is a SERP-first content engine for any brand. It crawls your brand, monitors trending topics, analyses the Google algorithm, and generates 5 fully optimised blogs every morning - ready to publish without editing.
Every blog is structured to rank #1 on Google by default. INK applies SEO, AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation), and GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) layers so your content surfaces in traditional search, AI Overviews, and tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity.
Getting started
How do I set up my workspace?
Enter your brand name, website URL, and LinkedIn company page. INK will crawl all three to build your content DNA - including tone, keywords, products, and positioning.
How do I get my 5 daily blogs?
Once autonomous mode is enabled in Settings, INK generates 5 blogs every morning at 04:00 IST. They appear in Today's Queue ready to publish.
How do I write a blog on a specific topic?
Go to Write on Demand, type your topic, complete the 4-step wizard, then click "Run Research & SERP Scan". INK generates a fully structured blog in under 60 seconds.
Competitor Intelligence
Add up to 5 competitor domains, click Analyse, and INK fetches their blog frequency, content themes, keyword focus, and positioning gaps using genuine web data.
Understanding scores
SERP score (0-100)
Rates how well a blog is optimised to rank on Google. Considers keyword density, heading structure, E-E-A-T signals, content depth, FAQ schema. 85+ is high-performing.
AEO score
Rates eligibility for Google's AI Overviews. Considers FAQ blocks, cited sources, clear answer formatting, author attribution. 80+ means likely to surface in AI answers.
GEO score
Rates how likely your content is to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude. Considers factual density, expert attribution, structured data.
